『Light Reboot』
The 2020 Taipei International Conference on Animation and New Media

　“Light Reboot” is the theme of the 2020 Taipei International Conference on Animation and New Media. The conference will be held on Friday, October 30, 2020 at the International Conference Hall in the 10th Floor of Teaching and Research Building in National Taiwan University of Arts. There will be keynote speeches, art/research paper presentations as well as animation and new media works debut in the program. Keynote speakers from the industry and the academia are invited to share their expertise and experiences to enrich the field of animation and New Media. Researchers and artists will gather together to present their works. We are pleased to invite all students, artists, scholars, and industry experts to participate in the conference.

Registration and Recipient Information

  • Registration：
    1. Fill in the online registration form at the following website before the registration deadline：https://ppt.cc/fZmTgx
    2. Registration is not complete until you receive a confirmation E-mail.
    3. The thesis will be submitted online. To use the file upload form, you need to log into your Google account. If you do not have a Google account, please send your email to ntuamaa@mail.ntua.edu.tw.
  • Material preparation information:
    1. Contact Information, Payment of documents, Letter of Authorization.( Appendix 3)
    2. Other materials to be paid. (Please refer to the details of item 7, range and specifications" for each category.)
    3. For the first time, the 2020 seminar will be conducted online, using Google forms. Submissions will not be open to mail, please kindly understand.
    4. Contribution of the dissertation should be the actual paper received as evidence. To accelerate the audit operation, when sending paper information, please register your account and fill out the online data sheet before sending paper information.
